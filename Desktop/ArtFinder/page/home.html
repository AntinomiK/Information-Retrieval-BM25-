About Us

Our website is a multi-contributor platform developed by two BDIC students. It aims to provide a fair and impartial platform for artists, photographers and artists to solicit contributions. You can recharge the virtual currency of the web page, choose your favorite art works directly place an order.

 We hope that buyers and sellers can fully communicate during the transaction, so as to ensure a good experience for both sides. At the same time, we provide an artist community where artists can communicate, discuss and create together
